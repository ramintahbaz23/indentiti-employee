Logging into the Portal

Learn how to access Identiti.Connect with your credentials and reach your home dashboard.

Overview

This guide will walk you through logging into the Identiti.Connect portal and reaching your home dashboard. You'll learn how to access the portal URL, enter your credentials, set up your password, and navigate to your dashboard.

Step-by-Step Guide

  • 1
    Open the portal URL
    Navigate to the Identiti.Connect URL provided by your Corporate Manager. This is typically a secure link sent via email or shared through your company's internal communication channels.
  • 2
    Enter your credentials
    Type your email address and temporary password in the login fields, then click the Log In button. Your email should be the one associated with your company account.
  • 3
    Set a permanent password
    On your first login, you'll be prompted to create a new password. Make sure it meets the security requirements (typically 8+ characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters). You may also need to verify your email address.
  • 4
    Reach your dashboard
    After successful login, you'll land on your home dashboard. This is your main hub where you can see work orders, notifications, and quick access to key features. Bookmark the URL for quick access in the future.

Tip

Using SSO?
If your company uses Single Sign-On (SSO), click the "Login with SSO" button and enter your corporate email address. You'll be redirected to your company's identity provider to authenticate, then automatically logged into Identiti.Connect.
